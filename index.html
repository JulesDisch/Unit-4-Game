Crystal Game

  • You are given a random target number at the start of the round.
  • Clicking on a crystal adds a specific amount of points to your current number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • You win the game by matching your current number to the target number; you lose the game if your total score goes above the target number.
  • Each time you win or lose a round, you have the option of playing another round.
  • If you choose to play another round, you will get a new target number, and new values for each crystal.
  • If you choose to stop playing, you won't get a new target number.
